Prop 30


Proposition 30, also known as Gov. Jerry Brown's tax initiative, is a measure that would increase sales and income taxes in order to restore funding to California's education system. If passed, Prop 30 will increase sales tax by 0.25% for four years and introduce a targeted income tax increase on households earning more than $250,000 a year. If it fails to pass, the University of California and California State University systems will get cut $250,000. The UC would immediately increase tuition by at least 20%, and the CSU would also raise tuition in addition to admitting 20,000 fewer students. Community colleges would also experience cuts, and teachers and faculty could expect more job losses and furloughs.
